Decrypting the 'Ghost in the Wires' ciphers

Last update on November 2019.

Before I was even done reading the amazing and classic Ghost in the Wires, I had to start deciphering the "quizzes" that introduce each chapter.

I know there are probably millions of solutions online, but I really wanted to see if I could solve them on my own.

During the next few weeks I hope I can find more and more solutions.
